With love

The Beauty of Ceramics. Beauty is everywhere. All you have to do is look for it. However, I find beauty most interesting when it comes in the purest and natural form as in nature. Nature is an infinite source of beauty. It is beautiful and forever changeable, each part and everyone is unique. It is a large treasury and archive of materials, which appeal to my aesthetics sense. I am particularly interested in surfaces and textures and how light can change the expression of the perceived.
